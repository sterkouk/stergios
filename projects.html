Thesis

Title: Video analysis by traffic cameras and vehicle identification for applications in intelligent transport systems

Artificial intelligence is playing an increasingly important role in our daily lives. Aiming to automate many processes in order to make everyday life easier and simplify many time-consuming and complex processes. An important field of where artificial intelligence develops rapidly is Vehicle Recognition, which can be integrated into different applications but all of those fall into the same category of creating a smart traffic network. The most modern and reliable method used is the Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN), which export the structural and spatial features of images in order to identify vehicles. In this paper firstly a Dataset of images is created and a CNN network is trained on it, with the purpose of extracting as valid information as possible from the images. There are also three other well-known convolutional networks for the purpose of the study and also in order to train them in this Dataset. Furthermore there is a comparison between the different techniques to draw the appropriate conclusions.
